Lesson 1: Blender UI Intro

Objective: Get familiar with Blender’s main panels in under 5 minutes.

1. Quick Steps

  1. Open Blender → you’ll see the 3D Viewport in the center.
  2. On the left: T panel for tools; on the right: N for properties.
  3. Top bar: File / Edit menus + workspace tabs.
